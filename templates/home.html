BasketCase Overview

BasketCase is a modernized and visual take on NBA Statistics. BasketCase only uses player and team data from the 2022/2023 season, due to time constraints for the project.

How It Works

The players page brings you to a sortable table of all of the NBA players from the 2022/2023 season. You can then select and player to get more in depth analysis on them. The same can be done in the Teams and Playoff pages. The graphs are interactive, allowing the user to toggle some features.

Introduction

Other sites provide great, in depth stats and allow for great analaysis. The only problem is they are often bland and sometimes hard to understand, such as basketball-reference. The goal of BasketCase is to make stats more accessible.

About

BasketCase and the database that backs it were created solely by Andrew Thompson. It is a final project for a Databases class. SQLite is the database the stats live in, and the stats were gathered directly from the NBA.
